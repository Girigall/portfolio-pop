How It Works
1. Robinhood app → Account → Reports and statements → generate activity report → download CSV.
2. Drop it here. The reader pairs fills into trades (FIFO per contract), computes realized P/L, and keeps only trades AFTER your ledger's last date — no doubles, ever.
3. Review the preview → download the updated trades_ledger.csv → replace the file in your Drive folder 05_Portfolio/history/. Done: the dashboard reflects it, and your files stay the source of truth.
Rows the reader can't pair confidently are marked needs_review — never guessed.
Note: reader P/L is net of exchange fees (your true cash), so figures may differ from broker-reported gains by a few dollars per trade.
